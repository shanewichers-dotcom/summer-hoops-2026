How this works
1. Each week: complete the Shooting DL blocks, Ball Handling drill, and Combo drill.
2. Shooting — practice using position A, B, or C all week. Submit scores from position A (behind the 3PT line) only.
3. You can practice all activities as many times as you want. Record your best scores by Sunday.
4. Log your scores in the Log Score tab and check the Leaderboard to see how you stack up.
5. Use the Explorer tab to record your preferred shooting form after each phase.
🤝 Be honest with your scores. Do not make up or exaggerate your numbers. Your coaches trust you and your teammates are counting on accurate results. Integrity matters.
